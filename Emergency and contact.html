When to Seek Help?

It's important to know when to reach out for professional help. If you're feeling overwhelmed, constantly anxious, sad for more than two weeks, or having thoughts of self-harm â€” you should not go through it alone. Support is available, and asking for help is a sign of strength.

Emergency Contact

In a mental health crisis, reaching out quickly can be life-saving. Universities usually offer free counseling services, and there are national hotlines that provide immediate help. Some websites also offer 24/7 mental health support through chat or phone.

Contact Form

If you're not ready to talk to someone face-to-face, you can send a message through this form. It's a private way to express how you're feeling and ask for help or more information about mental health services.
