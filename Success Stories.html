Overcoming Student Stress: Real Stories

Real students and individuals who overcame stress and mental health challenges.

Hannah Lucas – Overcoming Stress Through Innovation

Hannah Lucas, a high school student, faced severe anxiety and depression due to bullying and academic pressure. She worked with her younger brother to create a mobile app that allows users to quickly ask for help when they feel overwhelmed. Her journey shows how adversity can be turned into a positive force for change.

John Green – Turning Struggles into Stories of Hope

John Green, now a famous author, struggled with anxiety and obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD) during his college years at Kenyon College. By seeking therapy and support, he managed his mental health and later channeled his experiences into writing inspiring novels.

Chanelle McCoy – Finding Strength Through Community

Chanelle McCoy, a university student in the UK, struggled with severe stress and anxiety due to academic pressures. After seeking help through campus counseling services and participating in support groups, she found ways to manage her stress effectively. Chanelle now advocates for open conversations about mental health among students across universities.

Remember: Every challenge is an opportunity for growth. Never hesitate to seek help when you need it!
