You're Not Alone — Help is Here

Whether you're struggling with exams, feeling overwhelmed, or simply need someone to talk to, we’re here to support you. Our online counseling options are private, student-focused, and easy to access.

University Support

Many universities offer free or low-cost counseling services for students. Reach out to your campus wellness center or student affairs office to book a session with a licensed counselor.

Online Platforms You Can Trust

  • BetterHelp – Online therapy with licensed professionals, available via chat or video call.
  • Talkspace – Flexible, mobile-based counseling for students on the go.
  • 7 Cups – Free emotional support from trained volunteer listeners, available anytime.

Book an Appointment

Follow these simple steps:

  1. Choose a platform or your campus support system.
  2. Book an appointment or begin a chat session.
  3. Speak freely and receive support — it’s confidential and judgment-free.
